* About This Site
Right now, this is mostly a place for me to dump info as I tinker with making things for the GameBoy. Also, looking at what makes some of its games tick.

This site is created with GNU Emacs. I write the contents of the site in (mostly) org-mode, then use Org's publishing to do the rest. Check out [[https://systemcrafters.net/publishing-websites-with-org-mode/][the SystemCrafters guides]] for more information on getting started.

Absolutely *no* AI was used in the making of anything on this site. All of the intelligence displayed here, and lack thereof, is my own.
