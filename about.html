About Me

I'm an IT tech/sysadmin. I like to poke at things and figure out how they work. I write code sometimes, and every once in a while it actually works.

About This Site

Right now, this is mostly a place for me to dump info as I tinker with making things for the GameBoy. Also, looking at what makes some of its games tick.

This site is created with GNU Emacs. I write the contents of the site in (mostly) org-mode, then use Org's publishing to do the rest. Check out the SystemCrafters guides for more information on getting started.

Absolutely no AI was used in the making of anything on this site. All of the intelligence displayed here, and lack thereof, is my own.
